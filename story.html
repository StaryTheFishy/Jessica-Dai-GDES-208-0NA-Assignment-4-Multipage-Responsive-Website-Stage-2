About Us

Mugs for us is a webstore that is focused on making and selling mugs to our clients and customers, making the most top-notch and enjoyable mugs for anyone of any type and age to enjoy! No matter what, you will always be able to find a mug you'll like here!

Meet the Founders

John Doe

The founder of Mugs for US, John Doe had studied in business and financing, having gone on many business ventures, and many of them failing in the end. John then had the idea of starting a webstore for mugs, and with the help of his sister, he now has a successful business.

Jane Doe

The co-founder of Mugs for US, and sibling of John Doe, Jane Doe had studied arts and science at Humber College, and worked at pottery barn for 4 years, inspiring her brother to start a shop where they sell mugs, and helps in the production of making them.

Mary Sue

A well known friend of the founder John Doe and co-founder Jane Doe, Mary Sue had studied marketing and gotten a bachleor's degree, but had trouble finding work. She is in charge of marketing and advertising for the webstore, as well as promotional material.

Our Story

Mugs for us was founded by John Doe and co-founded by his sister Jane Doe back in 2022, after many failed business ventures from 2015-2021. They both eventually settled on making mugs, and decided to make it a business of theirs.

Our plans for the future

In the future, Mugs for us plans to make mugs that are made with more eco-friendly materials, while making a variety of them with different styles and designs, for anyone of any age to buy and enjoy their drinks from their very own mug!
